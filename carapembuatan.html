How to Make Ethylene Oxide

1. Manufacture of Ethylene Oxide from Ethylene and Oxygen

The reaction is run in the reactor fixed bed multitube at a pressure of 10 - 20 atm and temperature 220 - 280 o C by using silver catalyst. Conversions per pass kept low by about 15% to get high selectivity, which is 75%. In addition to forming ethylene oxide, also formed by product of CO2 gas and H2O with CO2 content tall one. This leads to the need for a CO2 circuit absorber and CO2 stripper to reduce CO2 content before the exit gas can be on recycle back into the reactor. In addition to preventing the effects of ethylene explosivity on oxygen, it is necessary to add nitrogen in the reactor cycle.
